# Free and Affordable Learning Resources for Rural Youth in India

## Government Online Learning Platforms

### SWAYAM (Study Webs of Active-Learning for Young Aspiring Minds)
- **URL**: https://swayam.gov.in
- **Description**: India's national MOOC (Massive Open Online Course) platform developed by MHRD. Hosts 1,000+ courses from IITs, IIMs, NITs, and central universities. Course videos are available completely free. A paid proctored exam (₹1,000–1,500) can be taken for a government-recognized certificate.
- **Credit Transfer**: UGC allows students to earn up to 40% of course credits through SWAYAM.
- **Best Courses**: Data Science, Machine Learning, Introduction to Programming, Financial Management, Rural Development, Agriculture, Engineering Mathematics
- **Language**: Hindi and English available for many courses

### NPTEL (National Programme on Technology Enhanced Learning)
- **URL**: https://nptel.ac.in
- **Description**: A joint initiative of IITs and IISc. Premier source of high-quality technical and science courses. All video lectures are free on YouTube and NPTEL website. Certificate exams can be taken at proctoring centers.
- **Best For**: Engineering students, science students, working professionals
- **Popular Courses**: Programming in Python, Data Structures, Machine Learning, VLSI Design, Environmental Science

### National Career Service Portal (NCS)
- **URL**: https://ncs.gov.in
- **Description**: Government of India's one-stop portal for employment and career-related services. Includes job listings, career counseling, career aptitude tests, skills assessment, and matching with skill development opportunities.
- **Free Services**: Resume building, career counseling sessions, aptitude tests
- **Job Database**: 3+ crore registered job seekers, 1+ lakh registered employers

### Skill India Digital Hub
- **URL**: https://skillindiadigital.gov.in
- **Description**: NSDC's unified digital platform for skill development. Lists PMKVY courses, links to training centers, online courses in 35+ sectors, and digital badges/certificates.
- **Free Courses**: Basic digital literacy, retail, healthcare, beauty, IT, agriculture

### PMGDISHA Portal
- **URL**: https://pmgdisha.in
- **Description**: PM Gramin Digital Saksharta Abhiyan — free digital literacy program for one rural household member per family who is digitally illiterate. 20-hour structured curriculum at nearest CSC (Common Service Centre).
- **Certificate**: Government-recognized digital literacy certificate
- **Best For**: First-time computer users, rural adults, senior citizens

### e-Basta
- **URL**: https://ebasta.in
- **Description**: Free digital textbooks for Classes 1–12 from NCERT and state boards. All books downloadable as PDF or accessible online.

### Diksha Platform
- **URL**: https://diksha.gov.in
- **Description**: National platform for school education. Free video lessons, practice worksheets, and assessments for Classes 1–12 aligned to CBSE and state curricula.

## International Free Learning Platforms (Accessible in India)

### Khan Academy
- **URL**: https://khanacademy.org
- **Description**: Completely free, non-profit educational platform with courses in Maths, Science, Computing, Economics, Arts, and Humanities from Class 1 to university level. Available in Hindi and other Indian languages.
- **Best For**: School and college students, self-learners, exam preparation (SAT, GMAT)
- **Internet Data**: Low-bandwidth mode available; 50 MB per hour

### Coursera (Audit Mode)
- **URL**: https://coursera.org
- **Description**: World's largest online learning platform with courses from Stanford, Google, IBM, Johns Hopkins, and more. Most courses can be "audited" for free (access to videos and assignments without a certificate). Financial aid available for certificates.
- **Best Courses for Rural Youth**: Google IT Support Certificate (free audit), IBM Data Science Certificate, Google Digital Marketing, CS50 from Harvard
- **Financial Aid**: Apply for financial aid to get certificates free if you cannot pay

### edX (Audit Mode)
- **URL**: https://edx.org
- **Description**: Similar to Coursera — courses from MIT, Harvard, Microsoft. Audit mode free.

### YouTube (Educational Channels)
- **URL**: https://youtube.com
- **Best Indian Educational Channels**:
  - **Khan Academy Hindi**: Mathematics in Hindi
  - **Physics Wallah**: Class 11–12 Physics and Chemistry (Hindi)
  - **Unacademy**: UPSC, SSC, Banking, JEE preparation
  - **Study IQ**: UPSC and government exam preparation
  - **Digital Defynd**: Career guidance and skill development
  - **TCS iON Career Edge**: Soft skills and professional development

### Google Skillshop
- **URL**: https://skillshop.google.com
- **Description**: Free certification courses in Google products: Google Ads, Google Analytics, Google Digital Marketing, Google Cloud, YouTube Content Creation. Certificates are industry-recognized.
- **Best For**: Marketing, digital advertising, cloud computing aspirants

### Microsoft Learn
- **URL**: https://learn.microsoft.com
- **Description**: Free learning paths for Microsoft Azure (cloud), Power BI, Microsoft 365, and GitHub. Industry certifications can be earned through exams.
- **Best For**: Cloud computing, data analysis, software development aspirants

### freeCodeCamp
- **URL**: https://freecodecamp.org
- **Description**: Free, open-source platform for learning web development, data science, machine learning. All courses entirely free with certification.
- **Best For**: Programming beginners, web developers
- **Certificates Available**: Responsive Web Design, JavaScript, Python, Data Analysis

## Competitive Exam Preparation Resources

### For UPSC (IAS/IPS/IFS)
- **Websites**: ForumIAS.com, Insights on India (insightsonindia.com)
- **YouTube**: Drishti IAS, StudyIQ, Unacademy UPSC
- **Free Materials**: NCERT Books (download from ncert.nic.in), NIOS Study Material
- **Official**: Yojana magazine (free digital edition), PIB (pib.gov.in), PRS India

### For SSC (CGL, CHSL, MTS)
- **Websites**: SSCAdda (sscadda.com), Oliveboard, TestBook
- **YouTube**: Wifistudy, Adda247
- **Books**: R.S. Aggarwal (Maths), Lucent's GK — available in local libraries and low-cost digital versions

### For Banking (IBPS PO, SBI PO, Clerk)
- **Websites**: BankersAdda (bankersadda.com), Oliveboard, Testbook
- **YouTube**: Adda247, Safalta Education
- **Free Mock Tests**: Most platforms offer 5–10 free mock tests

### For NEET (Medical)
- **YouTube**: NeetPrepDose, Aakash, Allen Career Institute
- **Free Resources**: NCERT Biology, Physics, Chemistry books are the primary source

### For JEE (Engineering)
- **YouTube**: Khan Academy Physics/Maths in English/Hindi, Physics Wallah
- **Free Mock Tests**: NTA's official practice portal, JEE Preparation App (NTA)

## Skill-Specific Resources

### Agriculture and Rural Development
- **Kisaan Suvidha App**: Real-time crop advisory, market prices, weather
- **mKisan Portal**: Agricultural advisories in local languages
- **e-NAM App**: Online agricultural marketplace
- **KVK (Krishi Vigyan Kendra)**: In-person free training at 732 KVKs across India — contact local KVK for schedule
- **ICAR e-Course**: Free e-learning courses from ICAR at ecoursesindia.com

### Healthcare and Paramedical
- **NISHTHA** (NCERT): Free teacher training also covers health education
- **Swasth Bharat Portal**: Health resources from MoHFW

### Financial Literacy
- **RBI Financial Education Portal**: https://rbi.org.in/financialeducation
- **SEBI Investor Education Portal**: https://investor.sebi.gov.in
- **NCFE (National Centre for Financial Education)**: Free courses at https://ncfeindia.org
- **PFRDA Pension Awareness**: Pension literacy for youth

### Legal Literacy
- **NALSA (National Legal Services Authority)**: Free legal aid and awareness
- **TeLaw**: Free legal education videos on YouTube

## Offline and Low-Connectivity Resources

### Common Service Centres (CSCs)
- 5+ lakh CSCs across India — can access all digital resources even without a personal device
- Services: SWAYAM access, scholarship applications, digital literacy, government scheme registration
- Locate nearest CSC: https://locator.csccloud.in

### District Libraries and Jan Shikshan Sansthan
- Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS): 272 institutes providing vocational training in local areas
- Free reading materials, competitive exam books, internet access
- Contact: Ministry of Education, Dept. of School Education

### eGram Swaraj Portal
- **URL**: https://egramswaraj.gov.in
- Gram Panchayat-level planning and development data — useful for understanding local opportunities
