# Indian Government Schemes for Rural Youth — Comprehensive Reference

## SKILL DEVELOPMENT SCHEMES

### PM Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY 4.0)
- **Full Name**: Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship
- **Implementing Agency**: National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC)
- **Target Beneficiary**: Youth aged 15–45 years, priority to school dropouts and rural youth
- **Benefit**: Free skill training of 150–300 hours across 300+ job roles. Stipend provided during training. Industry-recognized certification. Placement assistance.
- **Sectors Covered**: IT, agriculture, healthcare, construction, textiles, electronics, beauty, retail, banking, hospitality
- **How to Apply**: Visit nearest Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendra (PMKK) or register at www.skillindiadigital.gov.in
- **Official Website**: https://pmkvyofficial.org
- **Income Limit**: No income limit — free for all
- **Special Benefit**: Rural Youth track with focused placement assistance in home state

### SANKALP (Skills Acquisition and Knowledge Awareness for Livelihood Promotion)
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship
- **Benefit**: District Skill Development Plans (DSDPs) — skill development aligned to local industry demand
- **Target**: Rural districts, especially aspirational districts
- **Official Website**: https://sankalp.skillindiadigital.gov.in

### National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS)
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Skill Development
- **Benefit**: ₹1,500/month stipend during apprenticeship. 25% of stipend reimbursed to employer to encourage hiring.
- **Duration**: 6 months – 3 years
- **Eligibility**: Class 5 pass and above, aged 14–21
- **Official Website**: https://apprenticeshipindia.org

### Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) Construction Apprenticeship
- **Benefit**: Skill training in road construction for rural youth

## EDUCATION AND SCHOLARSHIP SCHEMES

### National Scholarship Portal (NSP) — Post-Matric Scholarships
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Education + Various ministries
- **Target**: SC/ST/OBC/Minority students
- **Benefit**: Full tuition waiver + maintenance allowance ₹3,000–10,000/year for residential students
- **Income Limit**: Family income below ₹2,50,000/year (most central schemes)
- **Eligibility**: Students who passed Class 10 and are pursuing Class 11, 12, diploma, graduation, or post-graduation
- **How to Apply**: https://scholarships.gov.in — apply online each academic year before deadline
- **Documents**: Caste certificate, income certificate, Aadhaar, bank account, last marksheet

### Central Sector Scheme of Scholarships (CSSS)
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Education
- **Target**: Top 20 percentile students in Class 12 state board
- **Benefit**: ₹12,000/year for first 3 years of graduation; ₹20,000/year for PG
- **Income Limit**: Family income below ₹8,00,000/year
- **Official Website**: https://scholarships.gov.in

### PM Scholarship Scheme (PMSS) — For Wards of Ex-Servicemen
- **Target**: Children of ex-servicemen/paramilitary personnel
- **Benefit**: ₹2,500/month (girls) and ₹2,250/month (boys)
- **Minimum Marks**: 60% in Class 12
- **Official Website**: https://desw.gov.in

### SWAYAM (Study Webs of Active-Learning for Young Aspiring Minds)
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Education
- **Benefit**: Free online courses from IITs, IIMs, and central universities. Credit transfer possible.
- **Courses**: 1,000+ courses in engineering, management, humanities, social sciences
- **Certificate**: Available for paid exam fee (₹500–1,000)
- **Official Website**: https://swayam.gov.in

### Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Education
- **Benefit**: Free textbooks, uniforms, midday meals, digital learning for Class 1–12 in government schools
- **Special**: Free hostel for tribal girls, transport allowance for girls in remote areas

### AICTE Pragati Scholarship for Girls
- **Target**: Girl students in AICTE-approved technical institutions
- **Benefit**: ₹50,000/year + contingency allowance
- **Income Limit**: Family income below ₹8,00,000/year
- **Official Website**: https://aicte-pragati-saksham-gov.in

### Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam IGNITE Scholarship
- **Target**: Class 9–12 students from disadvantaged backgrounds
- **Benefit**: ₹20,000 one-time grant for innovative project development

## ENTREPRENEURSHIP AND BUSINESS SCHEMES

### PM Mudra Yojana (PMMY)
- **Full Name**: Pradhan Mantri Micro Units Development and Refinance Agency
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Finance
- **Benefit**: Collateral-free business loans in three categories:
  - **Shishu**: Up to ₹50,000 (for startups and very small businesses)
  - **Kishore**: ₹50,001 – ₹5,00,000 (for growing enterprises)
  - **Tarun**: ₹5,00,001 – ₹10,00,000 (for established businesses)
- **Eligibility**: Non-corporate, non-farm small/micro enterprises. Any Indian citizen.
- **How to Apply**: Visit any PSB bank, microfinance institution, or mudra.org.in
- **Official Website**: https://mudra.org.in

### PM Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)
- **Ministry**: Ministry of MSME
- **Implementing Agency**: KVIC (Khadi and Village Industries Commission)
- **Benefit**: 15%–35% capital subsidy on project cost up to ₹25 lakh (manufacturing) or ₹10 lakh (service sector). Higher subsidy for SC/ST/Women/rural applicants.
- **Eligibility**: Age 18+, Class 8 pass for projects above ₹10 lakh
- **Official Website**: https://www.kviconline.gov.in/pmegpeportal

### Stand-Up India
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Finance
- **Target**: SC/ST entrepreneurs and women entrepreneurs
- **Benefit**: Bank loans ₹10 lakh – ₹1 crore for greenfield enterprises
- **Official Website**: https://www.standupmitra.in

### Startup India
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Commerce and Industry (DPIIT)
- **Benefit**: Tax exemption for 3 years, Fund of Funds (₹10,000 crore), fast-track patent processing, startup India Seed Fund (₹20 lakh – ₹50 lakh grant)
- **Eligibility**: DPIIT-recognized startups less than 10 years old
- **Official Website**: https://startupindia.gov.in

### Atal Innovation Mission (AIM)
- **Ministry**: NITI Aayog
- **Benefit**: Atal Tinkering Labs in schools, Atal Incubation Centres, grants up to ₹10 crore
- **Official Website**: https://aim.gov.in

## AGRICULTURE SCHEMES

### PM Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare
- **Benefit**: ₹6,000/year (₹2,000 every 4 months) direct to farmer bank account
- **Eligibility**: Small and marginal farmers with cultivable land. Family income criteria apply.
- **How to Apply**: PM-KISAN portal or nearest Common Service Centre (CSC)
- **Official Website**: https://pmkisan.gov.in

### Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Agriculture
- **Benefit**: Crop insurance at just 2% premium for kharif, 1.5% for rabi, 5% for commercial crops. Full sum insured paid for crop loss.
- **Official Website**: https://pmfby.gov.in

### Kisan Credit Card (KCC)
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Agriculture + RBI
- **Benefit**: Revolving credit up to ₹3 lakh at subsidized 4% interest rate for farm expenses
- **How to Apply**: Nearest bank branch or NABARD

### PM Kusum Yojana
- **Ministry**: Ministry of New & Renewable Energy
- **Benefit**: 60% subsidy on solar pumps for farmers. Farmers can sell surplus solar power to grid.
- **Official Website**: https://mnre.gov.in

### National Agriculture Market (eNAM)
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Agriculture
- **Benefit**: Online platform to sell agricultural produce directly at best prices, reducing middlemen
- **Official Website**: https://enam.gov.in

### Soil Health Card Scheme
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Agriculture
- **Benefit**: Free soil testing and soil health card with fertilizer recommendations for every farmer every 2 years

## FINANCIAL INCLUSION SCHEMES

### PM Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY)
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Finance
- **Benefit**: Free zero-balance bank account with RuPay debit card, ₹2 lakh accident insurance, ₹30,000 life insurance, overdraft up to ₹10,000
- **Official Website**: https://pmjdy.gov.in

### Atal Pension Yojana (APY)
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Finance (PFRDA)
- **Benefit**: Guaranteed pension ₹1,000 – ₹5,000/month after age 60. Government contributes 50% of contribution for first 5 years for new subscribers below poverty line.
- **Eligibility**: Age 18–40, bank account holder

### PM Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY)
- **Benefit**: ₹2 lakh life insurance at just ₹436/year premium
- **Eligibility**: Age 18–50, bank account

### PM Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY)
- **Benefit**: ₹2 lakh accidental insurance at ₹20/year premium
- **Eligibility**: Age 18–70, bank account

## HEALTH SCHEMES

### Ayushman Bharat — Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY)
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Health
- **Benefit**: ₹5 lakh/year cashless health insurance per family for secondary and tertiary hospitalisation
- **Eligibility**: SECC 2011 database families (approx. 12 crore poor families). Also extended to all families in some states.
- **How to Apply**: Automatic — check eligibility at https://pmjay.gov.in or nearest CSC
- **Official Website**: https://pmjay.gov.in

### Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY)
- **Target**: Pregnant women from below poverty line families
- **Benefit**: ₹600–1,400 cash assistance for institutional delivery in government hospitals

## HOUSING SCHEMES

### PM Awas Yojana — Gramin (PMAY-G)
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Rural Development
- **Benefit**: ₹1.20 lakh (plain areas) – ₹1.30 lakh (hilly/difficult areas) for construction of pucca house + ₹12,000 for toilet under SBM
- **Eligibility**: Houseless families or families with kachcha/dilapidated houses. SECC 2011 data.
- **Official Website**: https://pmayg.nic.in

## RURAL EMPLOYMENT AND LIVELIHOOD

### Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA)
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Rural Development
- **Benefit**: Guaranteed 100 days of wage employment per household per year. ₹200–300/day depending on state.
- **Eligibility**: Adult members of rural household willing to do unskilled manual work
- **How to Apply**: Register at Gram Panchayat office

### Deen Dayal Antyodaya Yojana — National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM)
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Rural Development
- **Benefit**: Formation of Self-Help Groups (SHGs), financial support, capacity building, revolving fund ₹10,000–15,000, Community Investment Fund, bank credit facilitation
- **Official Website**: https://aajeevika.gov.in

### PM SVANidhi (PM Street Vendor's AtmaNirbhar Nidhi)
- **Target**: Street vendors
- **Benefit**: Collateral-free working capital loan ₹10,000 (1st), ₹20,000 (2nd), ₹50,000 (3rd)
- **Official Website**: https://pmsvanidhi.mohua.gov.in

## DIGITAL LITERACY AND CONNECTIVITY

### PM Gramin Digital Saksharta Abhiyan (PMGDISHA)
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Electronics & IT
- **Target**: One person per rural household who is digitally illiterate
- **Benefit**: Free 20-hour digital literacy training at nearest Common Service Centre (CSC). Certificate recognized nationally.
- **How to Apply**: Visit CSC or register at https://pmgdisha.in
- **Official Website**: https://pmgdisha.in

### BharatNet
- **Ministry**: Ministry of Communications
- **Benefit**: High-speed broadband connectivity to all 2.5 lakh Gram Panchayats. Free/subsidized internet at Gram Panchayat level.

## STATE-SPECIFIC NOTES

### Uttar Pradesh
- **Mukhyamantri Yuva Swarozgar Yojana**: ₹25 lakh loan at 25% subsidy for youth entrepreneurs
- **UP Scholarship**: Strong SC/ST/OBC scholarship program through NSP + state portal

### Bihar
- **Bihar Student Credit Card**: ₹4 lakh education loan at 4% interest for higher education
- **Mukhyamantri Nishchay Swayam Sahayata Bhatta Yojana**: ₹1,000/month allowance for graduates seeking employment

### Rajasthan
- **Rajasthan Skill and Livelihoods Development Corporation (RSLDC)**: State skill training in 100+ trades
- **Mukhyamantri Yuva Kaushal Yojana**: Free skill training + job placement

### Maharashtra
- **Mahatma Jyotirao Phule Jan Arogya Yojana**: ₹1.5 lakh health insurance
- **Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Shikshan Shulkh Scholarship**: Tuition fee waiver for OBC students

### Tamil Nadu
- **Dr. Ambedkar Law Scholarship**: Full scholarship for SC students in law
- **CM's Adi Dravidar Welfare Scholarship**: SC students Class 6 onwards

### Madhya Pradesh
- **Mukhyamantri Medhavi Vidyarthi Yojana**: Full fee waiver for students scoring 70%+ in Class 12 (MP Board) in state colleges
- **Ladli Laxmi Yojana 2.0**: Financial support for girls including education grants

### Jharkhand
- **Mukhyamantri Protsahan Yojana**: ₹5,000/month to unemployed youth with graduation degree
- **Jharkhand e-Kalyan Portal**: State scholarship portal for tribal and backward class students

### West Bengal
- **Kanyashree Prakalpa**: Scholarship for girl students to prevent early marriage and support education
- **Jai Bangla Scholarship**: OBC-A and OBC-B category students

### Gujarat
- **Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Awas Yojana**: Housing for SC families
- **Mukhyamantri Apprenticeship Scheme**: Apprenticeship stipend top-up

### Karnataka
- **Rajiv Gandhi Scholarship for SC/ST**: Covers PG and professional education costs
- **CM's Breakfast Scheme**: Free breakfast in government schools
